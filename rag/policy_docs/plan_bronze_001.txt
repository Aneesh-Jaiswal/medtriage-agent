PLAN_BRONZE_001 - Coverage Policy (Synthetic, for demo purposes only)

Plan Tier: Bronze
Prior Authorization Required For:
- All emergency room visits except high-risk presentations
- All imaging studies
- All specialist visits
- Any procedure with an estimated cost over $200

Prior Authorization NOT Required For:
- High-risk emergency presentations only (chest pain, stroke symptoms, severe
  trauma, active labor, loss of consciousness)

Coverage Notes:
- Bronze tier members have a $3,000 deductible and 60% coinsurance after deductible.
- Low-risk cases are frequently denied prior authorization and redirected to
  urgent care or telehealth instead of ER.
- Medium-risk cases require escalation to a human reviewer; auto-approval is
  not permitted for this tier.
