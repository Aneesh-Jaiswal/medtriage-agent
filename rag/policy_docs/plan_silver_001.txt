PLAN_SILVER_001 - Coverage Policy (Synthetic, for demo purposes only)

Plan Tier: Silver
Prior Authorization Required For:
- All non-high-risk emergency room visits
- Advanced imaging (MRI, CT, PET)
- Specialist referrals outside of primary care network
- Durable medical equipment over $500

Prior Authorization NOT Required For:
- High-risk emergency presentations (chest pain, stroke symptoms, severe trauma,
  suspected fractures with visible deformity)
- Annual wellness visits

Coverage Notes:
- Silver tier members have a $1,000 deductible and 80% coinsurance after deductible.
- Requests for medium-risk cases require documentation of symptom duration and
  a physician recommendation before approval.
- Denials may be appealed within 30 days with additional clinical documentation.
