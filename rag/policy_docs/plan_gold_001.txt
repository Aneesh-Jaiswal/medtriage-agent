PLAN_GOLD_001 - Coverage Policy (Synthetic, for demo purposes only)

Plan Tier: Gold
Prior Authorization Required For:
- Emergency room visits classified as non-life-threatening (low/medium risk)
- Elective surgical procedures
- Advanced imaging (MRI, CT) for non-trauma indications

Prior Authorization NOT Required For:
- High-risk emergency presentations (chest pain, stroke symptoms, severe trauma)
- Routine primary care visits
- Prescription refills for chronic maintenance medications

Coverage Notes:
- Gold tier members have a $250 deductible and 90% coinsurance after deductible.
- Out-of-network emergency care is covered at in-network rates for high-risk cases.
- Follow-up visits within 30 days of an ER visit do not require separate authorization.
